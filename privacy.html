1. Introduction

PrimeNova ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services (graphic design, web development, mobile app development, cloud migration), or interact with our products (EduPrime ERP, NovaMart Pro).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number, and contact details
  • Business information, company details, and project requirements
  • Payment information and billing details for our services
  • Communication preferences and marketing consents
  • Professional background and technical specifications

2.2 Technical Information

  • IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system
  • Website usage data, page views, and navigation patterns
  • Cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies
  • Server logs, error reports, and performance metrics
  • Geolocation data (with your consent)

2.3 Service-Specific Data

  • EduPrime ERP: Student records, academic data, fee information, attendance records (processed on behalf of schools as data processor)
  • NovaMart Pro: E-commerce transaction data, inventory information, M-Pesa payment details, customer profiles
  • Development Projects: Project specifications, design assets, source code, deployment credentials
  • Cloud Migration: Infrastructure data, migration logs, performance analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our digital services and products
  • Process payments, manage billing, and handle client relationships
  • Communicate about projects, updates, support, and marketing (with consent)
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience and service quality
  • Ensure security, prevent fraud, and protect against unauthorized access
  • Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Conduct research and development for new features and services

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

  • Contract Performance: Processing necessary to fulfill our service agreements
  • Legitimate Interest: Improving services, security, and business operations
  • Consent: Marketing communications and optional features
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws and regulations

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (PayPal, M-Pesa), hosting services (AWS), analytics tools (Google Analytics)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
  • Business Partners: With your explicit consent for specific collaborations
  • Successors: In case of merger, acquisition, or business transfer

6. Data Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud storage with AWS with encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-factor authentication and access controls
  • Employee training on data protection and security best practices
  • Incident response procedures and breach notification protocols

7. Your Rights and Choices

Under applicable data protection laws (GDPR, Kenya Data Protection Act), you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: For processing based on consent

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Kenya, including:

  • United States (AWS servers, Google Analytics)
  • European Union (various service providers)
  • Other countries where our service providers operate

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses and adequacy decisions.

9. Data Retention

  • Client Data: Retained for the duration of our business relationship plus 7 years for legal compliance
  • Project Files: Retained for 3 years after project completion
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized after 26 months
  • Marketing Data: Until you withdraw consent or 3 years of inactivity

10. Children's Privacy

While EduPrime ERP processes student data on behalf of schools, we do not directly collect information from children under 16. Schools using our system are responsible for obtaining appropriate consents and complying with applicable laws regarding children's data.

11. Third-Party Services

Our website and services may contain links to third-party websites or integrate with third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services. Please review their privacy policies separately.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of material changes via email or website notice at least 30 days before they take effect.
