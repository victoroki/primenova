1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience, understand how you interact with our services, and improve our website's functionality and performance.

Cookies contain information that is transferred to your device's hard drive and help us recognize your device when you return to our website. They do not typically contain any information that personally identifies you, but personal information we store about you may be linked to the information stored in and obtained from cookies.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several important purposes:

  • To ensure our website functions properly and securely
  • To remember your preferences and settings
  • To analyze how visitors use our website and improve user experience
  • To deliver relevant advertising and marketing content
  • To provide customer support through chat widgets
  • To process payments securely for our services

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of our website and cannot be disabled:

  • Session Management: Keep you logged in during your visit
  • Security: Protect against cross-site request forgery (CSRF) attacks
  • Load Balancing: Distribute traffic across our servers for optimal performance
  • Form Handling: Remember form data and prevent duplicate submissions
  • Shopping Cart: Maintain items in your cart across pages (NovaMart Pro)
  • Authentication: Secure login for client portals (EduPrime ERP, NovaMart Pro)

6. Cookie Management and Your Choices

6.1 Browser Cookie Settings

You can control cookies through your web browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block all cookies (may affect website functionality)
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Set notifications when websites want to store cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close the browser

Browser-Specific Instructions:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

7. Cookie Retention Periods

  • Session Cookies: Automatically deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for specified periods (1 day to 2 years)
  • Essential Cookies: Typically last for the duration of your session
  • Analytics Cookies: Usually retained for 13-26 months
  • Marketing Cookies: Generally kept for 3-12 months
  • Functionality Cookies: Stored for 1-12 months depending on the feature

8. Impact of Disabling Cookies

While you can browse our website with cookies disabled, certain functionality may be limited:

8.1 Website Functionality

  • Login sessions may not persist between pages
  • Preferences and settings may not be saved
  • Shopping cart contents may be lost
  • Forms may not work properly

8.2 User Experience

  • Personalized content and recommendations may not appear
  • Website performance may be slower
  • You may see repeated content or advertisements
  • Analytics data may be incomplete

8.3 Service-Specific Impact

  • EduPrime ERP: Users may need to log in repeatedly, dashboard customizations won't save
  • NovaMart Pro: Shopping cart won't persist, payment processing may be affected
  • Customer Support: Chat history and context may be lost
